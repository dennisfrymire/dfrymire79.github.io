Listen...this game is pretty straight forward: There are five rounds of play, 10 questions each. Each correct answer is worth the number of points corresponding with its round. (Round 1 questions are worth one point, Round 2 questions are worth two, and so on.)

The questions get progressively harder in each round.

After every round, your score will be tallied. Get three or more questions wrong, and the game is over.

The first round is called Swiss-Cheesed Brain : These are general questions about the series that even the most casual fan should know.

When you're ready, activate the accelerator.
