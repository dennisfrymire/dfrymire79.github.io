Quantum Leap Quiz

Test your knowledge of the sci-fi cult classic TV show, Quantum Leap.

The clock starts when you hit Activate the Accelerator. You will have 30 seconds to answer the first question.

Thirty seconds will be added to the clock for every correct answer.

Fifteen seconds will be deducted for every incorrect answer.

As you progress through the game, the questions get tougher!

Answer 25 correct questions, and you win the game.

If the timer hit zero, you have lost. But keep coming back to play! This quiz has over 100 randomly generated questions.
